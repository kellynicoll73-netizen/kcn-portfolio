--- CALLOUT: FOCUS ON BRAND MANAGEMENT ---
callout-title: Focus on brand management
callout-challenge: Navigate both PwC and Salesforce brand requirements while creating memorable, PwC-branded experience that strengthens our position with potential buyers of our Salesforce-enabled services.
callout-approach:
- Strict adherence to Salesforce brand guidelines for all co-branded materials outside venue
- Negotiated special permission for subtle logo modifications (embroidery and textile textures) for co-branded deliverables remaining inside venue
- Positioned creative as strategic extension of existing PwC Canada back-office transformation campaign, to integrate the event into a broader customer journey
- Built stakeholder trust through proactive compliance planning
callout-result: Zero brand revisions from either PwC or Salesforce teams—enabling swift approval and protecting tight production timeline.

--- SECTION: SETTING UP CAMP ---
section-title: Setting up camp
section-subtitle: 25+ touchpoints, one unified experience
section-body: I created a cohesive experience across more than two dozen integrated touchpoints while navigating significant budget and timeline constraints. Every production decision was made with both creative impact and cost efficiency in mind, building on the existing transformation campaign to maximize what we already had, collaborating with the print vendor to find high-impact, low-cost methods, and using in-house facilities wherever possible.
subhead: Environmental transformation
bullets:
- Custom directional and informational signage (15+ pieces)
- Architectural column wraps to create "forest" atmosphere
- Branded food and beverage stations with thematic naming
- Window decals and atmospheric enhancements
- Coordinated with vendors and event management team to ensure that all visual and experiential details were delivered on-brand and in theme
subhead: Engagement and gamification
bullets:
- Merit badge collection system tied to activities showcasing PwC Canada's Salesforce services and products
- Event "passport" with activity tracking and contact information capture
- Thematic nametags and table toppers

--- CALLOUT: FOCUS ON PRODUCTION AND BUDGET MANAGEMENT ---
callout-title: Focus on production and budget management
callout-challenge: With six weeks remaining until event, custom embroidered merit badges would have consumed most of our production budget, with setup and approvals requiring more time than available.
callout-solution: Designed laser-cut stickers on durable, non-damaging vinyl substrate that mimicked embroidered aesthetic while maintaining premium collectible quality.
callout-result: Attendees not only completed their "adventure passports," many proudly wore badges on shirts and sleeves throughout the event—turning collectibles into conversation starters and attendees into visible brand ambassadors.
